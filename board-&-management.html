Board & Management

Clear roles, clear responsibilities.

We’ve organised our business to reflect our values of integrity and accountability, helping us work better together while meeting the highest standards of governance. Our board of directors comprises the chairman, three executive directors and seven independent non-executive directors. The board provides leadership to the Group and is responsible for its long-term success.

Our employees and contractors also play a key part in our governance, in line with our Code of Conduct and backed up by a confidential whistleblowing facility, YourVoice.

Chairman and CEO

Isaiah Ajang

CHIEF GEOLOGIST

Isaiah Ajang holds a bachelor of science in Geology from the University of Nairobi, Kenya. He is an Analyst on GIS and remote sensing and a researcher on Minerals exploration, oil and gas, Environment, Water and Sanitation. He has over 4 years‟ experience of consultancy in GIS and Remote sensing, Minerals Exploration, Environmental impact Assessment (EIA) and Social impact Assessment(SIA).Isaiah Ajang is a Chief geologist in Frontier Mining Ltd from January 2019 upto date.

Adil Anur

EXPLORATION GEOLOGIST

Adil Anur is a field and exploration Geologist at Frontier Mining Ltd, a pioneer Gold mining company in South Sudan.Before becoming at Frontier Mining Ltd,he serverd as a field Geologist at Gorom MiningLtd and successfully executed all the field exploration activities including underground water surveys.He also served as the operation manager at Frontier quarry Ltd. Adil earned BSc Geology and a certificate in GIS and remote sensing from the university of Nairobi.
