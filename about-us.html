COMPANY AT A GLANCE

Frontier Mining Ltd: Overview

Frontier Mining Ltd is a licensed junior mining company that is based in Juba, South Sudan. We have two small- scale licenses in the gold rich Luri Basin and one exploration license under a sister company.

We are a pioneer gold exploration and development company aimed at locating, developing and extracting a significant gold discoveries in Luri area and across South Sudan.The Luri Basin is part of the Achaean rocks of Congo craton known for hosting a world class gold deposit.
