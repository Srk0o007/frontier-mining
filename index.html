A Few Words About Our Mining Company

Frontier Mining Ltd is a licensed junior mining company that is based in Juba, South Sudan. We have two small- scale licenses in the gold rich Luri Basin and one exploration license under a sister company. In order to ensure the quality of our work, Frontier Mining has a quality assurance process. That means, amongst many other things, we have senior experts, independent of those responsible for a project and their role is to checks the result and delivery, from a quality point of view.

We are a pioneer gold exploration and development company aimed at locating, developing and extracting a significant gold discoveries in Luri area and across South Sudan.The Luri Basin is part of the Achaean rocks of Congo craton known for hosting a world class gold deposit.
